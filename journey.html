Our Path

The Enlightenment

During times of great challenges,
emerges a search to discover purpose.

Two major events: the Covid-19 global crisis, along with the civil upheaval in Myanmar from 2020 - 2021 acted as powerful triggers for her spiritual awakening, inspiring her to help those around her. Her path as a mystical card reader gained momentum through personal referrals during these challenging times.

The Origin

Starting phase of
the Path on Facebook

She launched "Truth Always Reveals" as a private Facebook community, but the openness and sharing spirit of its members enabled it to evolve into a broader spiritual awakening network. Although their mystical journeys vary, they managed to exchange experiences, wisdom, and insights within this exclusive circle. Mystical card interpretations might have been the first step on this path, but it has expanded to include energy restoration methods, ancient symbol readings, celestial and natal chart analysis, spiritual practices, and learning universal languages and mentorship. Thus, she dedicates her time as a guide, interpreter, instructor, and healer for those seeking help in finding their own answers and solutions for their communities.

Who is she?

Creator's Profile

Maya Chen, developer of the mystical products of Sacred Vessel and card reader for Truth Always Reveals, started her journey in this field in 2020. She worked for more than ten years at a Singaporean primary school before embarking on her spiritual exploration. She lived many years in Singapore pursuing higher education after her childhood in Myanmar (Burma). During the worldwide crisis of 2020, she started her quest for meaning and deeper life purpose after obtaining two certificates, a bachelor's degree, and a master's in education.
