Subscription Benefits

English

Premium Plus and Premium Star Benefits

PP and PS members are entitled to a 30-minute interpretation or consultation session per month. PP and PS members must specify the type of interpretation or consultation desired when scheduling.

Session bookings are available for selection when logging in with subscription credentials.

Subscription login details will be delivered to members via email/messenger following monthly payment confirmation.

If a session date requires rescheduling, the existing booking must be canceled before creating a new appointment.

For single-inquiry responses, members may submit their written questions to the guide.

Payment Questions and Guidelines

Monthly subscription payment is mandatory each calendar month. For optimal consultation outcomes and progress tracking, continuous membership of at least 6 months is strongly advised for regular monthly consultation check-ups.

Payment confirmation must be forwarded to the email for validation.

Payment notifications will be sent to members one week prior to the due date.

If payment lapses more than twice within 6 months, membership may be suspended until further notice.

Premium Express and Premium Basic Benefits

PE and PB members are entitled to a 30-minute group consultation session every two months. PE and PB members must specify their preferred group consultation time slots through the members' login portal.

Session bookings are available for selection when logging in with subscription credentials.

Subscription login details will be delivered to members via email/messenger following monthly payment confirmation.

If a session date requires rescheduling, the existing booking must be canceled before creating a new appointment.

For next-day single-inquiry responses, members may submit their written questions to the guide. Responses will be delivered to the member within a week.

Payment Questions and Guidelines

Monthly subscription payment is mandatory each calendar month. For optimal consultation outcomes and progress tracking, continuous membership of at least 6 months is strongly advised for regular monthly consultation check-ups.

Payment notifications will be sent to members one week prior to the due date.

If payment lapses more than twice within 6 months, membership may be suspended until further notice.

Confidentiality Agreement

For a customized interpretation, these details and information will be gathered with strict confidentiality for the guide:

  1. Member's birth date
  2. Member's birth time and location
  3. Member's clear photograph without masks/glasses
  4. Member's complete home address (for shipping purposes)
  5. Member's active phone number(s)
  6. Member's working email address
  7. Member's preferred type of interpretation or consultation (may select multiple options)
  8. Member's desired process and results through the sessions with the guide
